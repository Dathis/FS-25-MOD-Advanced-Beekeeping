# Bee colony

## General Description

The **Bee Colony Box** is a portable item representing a live bee colony with a queen.

Its primary purpose is to **initialize a beehive** and enable honey production.

The item is consumable and disappears after being used in a beehive.

---

## Core Properties

The Bee Colony Box must have the following attributes:

- **Bee population:** 50,000 bees
- **Queen presence:** Yes (always included)
- **Weight:** 10 kg
- **Initial price:** 100 €
- **Type:** Portable item (not placeable)
- **Stackable:** No

---

## Purchase & Availability

- The player can purchase the Bee Colony Box in the **shop menu**
- It appears as a standard item (similar to tools or small objects)
- It is **not placeable in the world as a building**
- After purchase, it spawns as a physical object

---

## Player Interaction

The player must be able to:

- pick up the Bee Colony Box
- carry it manually
- transport it using vehicles (optional future extension)

---

## Usage

### Primary Function

The Bee Colony Box is used to **populate a beehive with bees**.

---

## Interaction with Beehive

When the Bee Colony Box is placed inside the **beehive interaction trigger area**, the following logic applies:

### Conditions for activation:

The colony is accepted only if:

- the beehive does **not already contain a bee colony**
- the beehive has **at least one wax frame installed**

---

### On successful interaction:

- the Bee Colony Box is **consumed (removed from the world)**
- the beehive receives:
    - bee population = 50,000
    - queen = present
- the beehive state updates:
    - from **Empty → HasFrames** (if frames were just added)
    - or from **HasFrames → Active**

---

### On invalid interaction:

If conditions are not met:

- the box is **not consumed**
- no changes occur

Examples:

- hive already has a colony → reject
- hive has no frames → reject (optional rule, но логично оставить)

---

## Behavior After Use

Once inserted into a valid beehive:

- the Bee Colony Box disappears completely
- it cannot be reused
- it does not leave behind an empty container (упрощаем)

---

## Limitations

- one hive can contain **only one bee colony**
- no partial usage (always full colony)
- no splitting or merging colonies (future feature)

---

## Visual & Feedback (Optional but recommended)

When used successfully:

- short animation or effect (bees flying into hive)
- sound effect (buzzing increase)
- UI message:
    - “Bee colony added”

When rejected:

- UI message:
    - “Hive already contains a colony”
    - “Insert frames first”

---

## Scope Boundary

The Bee Colony Box is responsible only for:

- delivering a bee population
- providing a queen
- activating the hive

It is **not responsible** for:

- honey production
- frame management
- bee health
- reproduction mechanics
