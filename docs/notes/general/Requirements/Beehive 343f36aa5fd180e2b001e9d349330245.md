# Beehive

## General Description

The **Beehive** is the main production unit of the mod.

Its purpose is to simulate the natural honey production process inside a hive by allowing bees to fill wax frames over time.

The beehive does **not** output honey directly.

Instead, it converts **empty wax frames** into **filled honey frames**, which must later be manually removed by the player and processed in the **Honey House**.

---

## Main Purpose

The beehive must:

- accept empty wax frames
- accept a bee colony
- start working only when fully prepared
- convert empty frames into filled honey frames over time
- allow the player to manually remove filled frames using a frame box
- stop functioning in unsuitable seasons

---

## Beehive States

The beehive has the following functional states:

### 1. Empty

The hive has no frames and no bee colony.

**Characteristics:**

- not working
- cannot produce anything
- waits for empty wax frames

---

### 2. HasFrames

The hive contains empty wax frames but has no bee colony yet.

**Characteristics:**

- not working
- ready to receive a bee colony

---

### 3. Active

The hive contains empty wax frames and a bee colony with a queen.

**Characteristics:**

- working
- gradually converts empty frames into filled honey frames
- displays active hive information to the player

---

### 4. Seasonal Inactive

The hive is correctly assembled but cannot work because of the current season.

**Characteristics:**

- no production
- keeps its internal contents
- becomes active again when the season allows it

This state applies during:

- **Autumn**
- **Winter**

Production is possible only during:

- **Spring**
- **Summer**

---

## Placement and Setup

### Buying and Placing

The player can buy the beehive in the shop and place it anywhere on their farm.

After placement, the hive is empty and non-functional.

---

## Interaction Trigger Area

Each beehive must have a small interaction area near it.

This trigger area is used for:

- inserting a box with empty wax frames
- inserting a bee colony box
- inserting an empty frame box for collecting filled frames

The interaction must be simple and mostly automatic once the correct item is placed inside the trigger.

---

## Loading Empty Frames

The player must bring a **Box with Wax Frames** to the hive.

When the box is placed in the hive trigger area:

- the empty frames are automatically transferred into the hive
- the hive updates its internal frame count
- the box is either emptied or consumed, depending on implementation choice

If the hive now contains frames but still has no bee colony, its state changes to **HasFrames**.

---

## Adding the Bee Colony

The player must bring a **Bee Colony** box to the hive.

When the colony box is placed in the hive trigger area:

- the bee colony is automatically transferred into the hive
- the colony box disappears / is consumed
- the hive becomes active if all required conditions are fulfilled

### Requirements for activation:

The hive starts working only if:

- it contains at least one empty wax frame
- it contains a bee colony
- the colony has a queen
- the current season is Spring or Summer

---

## Internal Hive Data

The beehive should internally track at least the following values:

- bee population
- queen presence
- empty frame count
- filled frame count
- maximum frame capacity
- current state
- seasonal activity status

Example:

- Bee population: 12,000
- Queen: present
- Empty frames: 7
- Filled frames: 5
- Max capacity: 12

---

## Honey Frame Production

The hive does not produce honey as a liquid output.

Instead, it fills wax frames over time.

### Production logic:

While the hive is active:

- bees gradually process empty frames
- over time, empty frames become filled honey frames

### Requirements for production:

Production only happens when:

- the hive is active
- the queen is present
- there is at least one empty frame available
- the season is Spring or Summer

### Production stop conditions:

Production stops when:

- all frames are filled
- no bee colony is present
- no queen is present
- the season changes to Autumn or Winter

---

## Collecting Filled Frames

When the hive contains filled honey frames, the player must manually collect them.

### Collection process:

1. The player brings an **empty Box for Frames**
2. The player places it in the hive trigger area
3. The hive automatically transfers filled honey frames into the box
4. The player receives a box containing filled frames

The hive does not output anything automatically without player interaction.

This means:

- no honey pallets
- no liquid honey
- no direct sale output
- only removable filled frames

---

## Frame Replacement Cycle

After collecting filled frames, the player has two possible options:

### Option 1: Return the extracted frames later

After processing in the Honey House, the player may bring back empty frames and insert them into the hive again.

### Option 2: Insert new empty frames

The player may also use another box with new empty wax frames.

This creates the main gameplay loop:

- insert empty frames
- add bee colony
- wait until frames are filled
- collect filled frames into frame box
- process them elsewhere
- return empty frames or add new ones

---

## Hive Information Display

When the player looks at the hive, the following information should be shown:

- hive status
- bee population
- queen presence
- number of empty frames
- number of filled frames

### Example UI text:

- Status: Working
- Bees: 12,000
- Queen: Yes
- Empty Frames: 6
- Filled Frames: 6

Possible statuses:

- Empty
- Waiting for Frames
- Waiting for Bee Colony
- Working
- No Queen
- All Frames Filled
- Inactive (Autumn/Winter)

---

## Capacity Rules

The hive must have a fixed maximum frame capacity.

Example:

- maximum 12 frames

Rules:

- the hive cannot accept more than the maximum capacity
- filled and empty frames together cannot exceed the total capacity
- production stops when all frame slots are occupied by filled frames
